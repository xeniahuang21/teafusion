About Oolong

SUPPORTS VITALITY

Oolong tea is a type of tea that falls somewhere between green tea and black tea in terms of oxidation. It is made from the leaves of the Camellia sinensis plant, the same plant that is used to produce black, green, and white teas.

The leaves of oolong tea are partially oxidized, meaning they are allowed to wilt in the sun and undergo a process of fermentation before they are heated and dried. This process gives oolong tea its distinctive flavor and aroma, which is often described as a combination of the fresh, grassy notes of green tea and the rich, earthy tones of black tea.

HEALTH BENEFITS

Our traditional Oolong Tea is composed of a unique blend of semi-oxidized leaves, leaving a smooth, savory taste. Oolong is known for supporting vitality, improving gut microbiome, and protecting brain function.

HEART HEALTH

Drinking oolong tea may help lower blood pressure and reduce the risk of heart disease, thanks to its antioxidants and other beneficial compounds.

IMPROVED MENTAL ALERTNESS

Oolong tea contains caffeine, which can improve focus and alertness, making it a great choice for people who need a mental boost.

BETTER DIGESTION

Oolong tea can help stimulate the digestive system and reduce inflammation in the gut, leading to better digestion and a healthier gut microbiome.
